Who We Are

Nyan Cow is a Diablo 4 clan built around actually playing together, not just sharing a tag. Expect a mix of chill and chaotic — plenty of banter, plenty of "one more Pit run," and enough dark humor to match the game.

We deliberately split groups instead of letting one clique run everything. If a group gets too full or too stacked, we break it up so more people get playtime with more people. Nobody sits on the bench for long — that's a house rule, not a suggestion.

About Nyan Cow

Who we are and how we play.

Our Vibe

We're here to actually play together, not just share a tag. Expect a mix of chill and chaotic — plenty of banter, plenty of "one more Pit run," and enough dark humor to match the game.

How We Group

We deliberately split groups instead of letting one clique run everything. If a group gets too full or too stacked, we break it up so more people get playtime with more people. Nobody sits on the bench for long — that's a house rule, not a suggestion.

New Around Here?

Jump in the Discord, say hi, tell us your class and roughly how much you play. Check the roster spotlights to get a feel for who's around, and the build guides if you want a head start on gearing.
